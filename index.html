Connect Four

Red player's turn

Instructions

How to play

Two players, the Red player and the Yellow player, take turns placing markers on the board. The first player to place four markers of their color in a line wins. Lines can match horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Controls

Players can place markers by clicking (desktop) or tapping (mobile) on the column they wish to place their marker. Additionally, on desktop, columns can be tabbed through and the player can press enter or space to place a marker.
